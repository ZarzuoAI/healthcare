Patient Appointment Reminder
Appointment System → WhatsApp Business → SMS
₹25K–₹50K
Monthly Value
↓
The Problem

Every no-show is a blocked appointment slot that could have served a paying patient. Across a 30-doctor clinic, even 3–4 no-shows per doctor per week translates to ₹5–12 lakhs in lost annual revenue — with staff still burning time on manual reminder calls that often don't work anyway.

The Solution

An automated, multi-touch reminder system that sends a WhatsApp message 48 hours before, an SMS 4 hours before, and a confirmation link that lets patients reschedule instantly — freeing up the slot for someone else without any staff involvement.

  • Step 01New appointment is booked — our automation engine is notified instantly in the background
  • Step 02A personalised WhatsApp reminder is sent 48 hours before — with the patient's name, doctor, and appointment time
  • Step 03An SMS backup is sent 4 hours before, in case the patient missed the WhatsApp message
  • Step 04Patient taps one button to confirm, reschedule, or cancel — the appointment slot updates automatically, no staff needed
  • Step 05If the patient still doesn't show, they are automatically flagged for a re-booking follow-up call
↓ 40–60% no-show reduction 0 staff hours on reminders Auto slot recovery Works 24/7

The Problem

Manual billing creates a predictable chain of failure: data entry errors, delayed invoices, missed follow-ups on unpaid bills, and disconnected records between your billing and accounts teams. For a mid-size hospital, this quietly costs ₹5–15 lakhs every year in revenue leakage.

The Solution

The moment a patient visit is complete, billing records sync automatically between systems, an invoice is generated and sent via WhatsApp and email, and payment follow-ups are scheduled without any manual intervention.

  • Step 01Patient is discharged — the billing record is created automatically in your billing software, no manual entry needed
  • Step 02Our automation engine syncs this data to your accounts system and generates the invoice in seconds
  • Step 03Invoice is sent to the patient via WhatsApp and email at the same time — clear, professional, instant
  • Step 04If the payment is pending, automated reminders go out on Day 3, Day 7, and Day 14 — politely, without staff follow-up
  • Step 05Once payment is received, a receipt is sent and both billing and accounts systems are updated — fully reconciled, automatically

The Problem

The Solution

  • Step 01Lab technician marks the report as complete in your lab system
  • Step 02Our automation engine picks this up immediately — no delay, no manual action required
  • Step 03Patient receives a personalised WhatsApp message informing them their report is ready, with a secure link to view it
  • Step 04If the WhatsApp message is undelivered within 30 minutes, an SMS is sent automatically as a backup
  • Step 05If the report has critical values, a prompt is automatically sent to book a follow-up consultation
